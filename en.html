Research and Development (R&D)
Initiative Supporting innovative research projects

?What is R&D Initiative in Jouf University

R&D initiative aims to support innovation by supporting innovative research projects in order to introduce and develop innovative services and products based on knowledge. These need to be in line with the priorities of Innovation Strategic Plan of Jouf University. Innovative research is systematic work, drawing on existing knowledge gained from research and/or practical experience, which is directed to producing new materials, products or devices, to installing new processes, systems and services, or to improving substantially those already produced or installed.

Objectives

1

Supporting the university’s research and innovation system to contributes in achieving the Kingdom’s Vision 2030 in building a knowledge society and a knowledge-based economy.

2

Stimulating and supporting creative ideas.

3

Transforming scientific research into products of economic value.

4

Raising the rank of Al-Jouf University in the indicators of innovation locally and internationally.

R&D Conditions

R&D Conditions

1

To be the principal investigator from the employees of Al-Jouf University

2

Publish in domains classified within SCOPUS or WOS databases

3

Priority shall be given to applied projects with innovative outputs Priority for research that falls within the university’s research priorities shall be: - Water desalination.
- Renewable energy.
- Financial technology.
- Health research.
- Cypersecurity.
- Olive research.
